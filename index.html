FAQ

Do you offer same-day service?

Yes—text photos to 438-529-7778 and we’ll confirm timing.

What don’t you take?

Hazardous waste (paint, chemicals, fuel) without prior approval. We can help route you to the right facility.

How do I get the best price?

Group items in one area and send clear, well-lit photos. Mention stairs/elevator/tight access.

Do you donate or recycle?

Whenever possible—we separate metals, electronics, and usable items for donation.
